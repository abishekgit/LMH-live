About Dermatology

Welcome to the Dermatology Department at Lalita Memorial Hospital. Our team of experienced dermatologists offers expert online consultations for a wide range of skin conditions. Whether you're dealing with acne, dermatitis, or other skin issues, we are here to provide personalized and convenient care.

At Lalita Memorial Hospital, we understand the importance of accessible dermatological care. Our online consultations allow you to connect with our dermatologists from the comfort of your home, ensuring timely and effective advice for your skin health.

Our Mission: To provide expert dermatological consultations online, promoting skin health and well-being for our patients.

Online Consultations: Our Dermatology Department offers virtual consultations, allowing you to discuss your skin concerns with our dermatologists remotely. This convenient service ensures timely access to expert advice without the need for an in-person visit.

Services Offered Online

Our online dermatology consultations cover a wide range of services, including diagnosis and treatment recommendations for common skin conditions, skincare advice, and guidance on managing dermatological concerns from the comfort of your home.
