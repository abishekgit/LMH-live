About Dr. Seema Mittal

Dr. Seema Mittal, a distinguished medical professional, is a leading Gynecology and IVF Specialist at Lalita Memorial Hospital Pvt Ltd. She holds a Gold Medal in MD and is recognized for her outstanding contributions in the field of Obstetrics and Gynecology.

With a rich background in infertility treatment, Dr. Seema Mittal has been dedicated to providing comprehensive care for women's health. Her expertise extends to Gynecological issues, and she is renowned for her proficiency in In Vitro Fertilization (IVF).

Awards and Recognitions

  • Past President of Rewari Obstetrics and Gynecology Society
  • Secretary at Doctors for Society
  • Treasurer at Haryana Indian Fertility Society
  • Awarded by the President of India for the book "Sukhada Matritva"
  • Recipient of the Local Champion Award by the Government of Haryana

Experience and Achievements

Dr. Seema Mittal is a renowned infertility specialist with a remarkable career dedicated to women's health and fertility. Her expertise and leadership have played a pivotal role in making Lalita Memorial Hospital the first IVF Centre in Haryana.

As a committed professional, she has contributed significantly to the medical community, serving as the Past President of Rewari Obstetrics and Gynecology Society and holding key roles in various medical societies.

Dr. Seema Mittal's commitment to women's health and her tireless efforts in infertility treatment have been recognized through numerous awards and accolades.
