About Us

Welcome to the Gynecology Department at Lalita Memorial Hospital, where we are committed to providing comprehensive and compassionate healthcare for women. Our dedicated team of gynecologists and support staff is here to address a wide range of women's health needs with expertise and care.

At Lalita Memorial Hospital, we understand the importance of women's health at every stage of life. Whether you require routine check-ups, family planning, or specialized care, our Gynecology Department is here to support you with personalized and patient-centered services.

Our Mission: To promote and maintain the health and well-being of women through comprehensive and personalized gynecologic care.

Our Services: Our Gynecology Department offers a wide range of services, including but not limited to:

  • Routine check-ups and screenings against Survical Cancer and Management, Breast Cancer Screening and Management
  • Family planning and contraceptive counseling
  • Prenatal and postnatal care
  • Menstrual health management
  • Laproscopic Gynecological surgeries
  • Menopause management
  • Managing Adolescent Health Problems
