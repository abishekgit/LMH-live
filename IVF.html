About Us

Welcome to the IVF Department at Lalita Memorial Hospital, where we are dedicated to helping individuals and couples achieve their dreams of parenthood. With a commitment to excellence, compassion, and cutting-edge fertility treatments, we strive to make the journey to parenthood a positive and successful experience. Ours being the 1st IVF centre in Haryana.

At Lalita Memorial Hospital, with more than 10,000 couples achieving parenthood we understand that the path to starting a family can be challenging, and we are here to provide personalized care and support at every step. Our team of experienced and compassionate fertility specialists, doctors, and support staff work together to create a warm and nurturing environment for our patients.

Our Mission: To empower individuals and couples by offering advanced and comprehensive fertility treatments while providing unwavering support and personalized care throughout their fertility journey.

Our Approach: We combine state-of-the-art technology with a patient-centered approach to fertility care. Our team of experts utilizes the latest advancements in reproductive medicine to tailor treatment plans that address the unique needs of each individual or couple.

Services

  • Fertility testing and diagnosis
  • Ovulation Induction
  • Reproductive Surgery (Laproscopy, Hysteroscopy)
  • In vitro fertilization (IVF)
  • ICSI
  • PESA, MESA
  • TESE,TESA
  • Embryo transfer
  • Embryo Freezing
  • Sperm Freezing
  • Donor Programs
