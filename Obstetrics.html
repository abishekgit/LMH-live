About Obstetrics

Welcome to the Obstetrics department at Lalita Memorial Hospital, where we are committed to providing comprehensive and compassionate care to women during pregnancy and childbirth. Our dedicated team of obstetricians and support staff is here to ensure the well-being of both mother and baby throughout this miraculous journey.

At Lalita Memorial Hospital, we understand that each pregnancy is unique and requires individualized attention. Our experienced obstetricians work closely with expectant mothers to monitor the health of both mother and baby, providing expert care and guidance at every stage of pregnancy.

Our Mission: Every pregrancy should culminate in a normal Delivery

Our Services: Our Obstetrics department offers a range of services, including GarbhSanskar and Pregnancy Yoga during prenatal period, delivery, postnatal care, and support for high-risk pregnancies. We prioritize the health and well-being of both the mother and the newborn.

Our Team

Meet our dedicated team of obstetricians, nurses, and support staff. Each member is committed to providing the highest level of care and support to expectant mothers, ensuring a positive and memorable childbirth experience.

Facilities
