Our Blood Bank

The Blood Bank at Lalita Memorial Hospital is fully equipped with the latest technology to ensure safe and efficient blood storage. We prioritize the well-being of our patients by maintaining high standards in blood storage and handling.

Our blood bank features a deep freezer capable of storing plasma for 1-5 years. The freeze can store up to 450 units of blood. We utilize the latest instruments for sealing and maintaining platelets, ensuring the quality of blood components.

The Apheresis Platelet Extractor machine in our blood bank is of Trima Japanese make. This machine is designed to remove platelets in less time, and its extracorporeal volume is lesser than other machines, making it more patient-friendly.

All Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and quality protocols are diligently followed as per national standards. Our blood bank is fully recognized for 5 years by blood bank authorities, reflecting our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of safety and quality.

Key Equipment

  • Deep Freezer for Plasma Storage [-40°C and -80°C]
  • Platelet Storage Freeze (Capacity: 450 units)
  • Workstation
  • Blood Monitors
  • Elisa Reader
  • Platelet-rich plasma (PRP)
  • Apheresis Platelet Extractor Machine (Trima Japanese Make)
  • All equipments related to Blood Donation Camps
