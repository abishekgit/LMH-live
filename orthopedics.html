About Orthopedics

Welcome to the Orthopedics Department at Lalita Memorial Hospital. Our team of skilled orthopedic surgeons and healthcare professionals is dedicated to providing comprehensive and personalized care for musculoskeletal conditions and injuries.

At Lalita Memorial Hospital, we understand the impact that musculoskeletal issues can have on daily life. Whether you're dealing with joint pain, fractures, or sports injuries, our Orthopedics Department is here to help you regain mobility and live a healthy, active life.

Our Mission: To minimize surgical procedures, providing pain clinic to minimize painful bony and muscular conditions.

Our Services: Our Orthopedics Department offers a wide range of services, including Arthroscopy, fracture management, sports medicine, and rehabilitation, joint replacement surgeries. We focus on patient-centered care to address individual needs and goals.

Our Team

Meet our experienced team of orthopedic surgeons, physiotherapists, and pain management and support staff. Each member is dedicated to providing compassionate care and helping patients achieve optimal musculoskeletal health.

Specialties

Our Orthopedics Department specializes in various areas, including joint replacement, spine surgery, and sports medicine. We tailor treatment plans to address specific orthopedic conditions and promote long-term wellness.

Contact Us

For appointments, consultations, or inquiries about our orthopedic services, please contact our Orthopedics Department. We are here to support you on your journey to improved musculoskeletal health.
